Chicken Burger

Description

Perfect for quick, casual, and filling meals.

Ingredients

  • 6 sweet potatoes; cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 6 hamburger buns; cut horizontally and buttered
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 large pineapple; skin removed and sliced into 5–8 mm thick rounds
  • 6 chicken breasts
  • 1 bottle of smoky BBQ sauce
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • A small head of lettuce; washed
  • Mayonnaise; to serve

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Rolling pin
  • Timer
  • Frying Pan

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 °C. Place sweet potato wedges on a roasting tray, then season with salt and pepper and drizzle with oil.
  2. Roast for 25 min. until golden brown and tender.
  3. Toast the buttered buns in a dry pan until golden brown, then set aside.
  4. Using the same pan, add some olive oil and fry the pineapple slices over high heat until caramelised on both sides. Set aside.
  5. Place the chicken breasts on a layer of cling film, then cover with another layer.
  6. Use a rolling pin to beat the breasts until they are about 1 cm thick.
  7. Fry the breasts in a little oil in the same pan on both sides, basting with some BBQ sauce during the last 2 min.
  8. Remove from the heat.
  9. Assemble the burgers by placing some lettuce and tomato on the bottom halves of each bun.
  10. Then top with mayonnaise, a chicken breast, grilled pineapple and the top half of the bun.
