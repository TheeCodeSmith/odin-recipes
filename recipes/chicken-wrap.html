chicken-wrap

Description

This Middle-Eastern inspired dish is a perfect for quick meals and is a playful twist on a traditional Chicken sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 cooked & shredded chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp. sour cream, plain yoghurt or mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp. pesto
  • 2 thin slices of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Handful of chopped red pepper or sweetcorn kernels

Equipment

    No Special Equipment required

Steps

  1. Mix the shredded chicken, sour cream, yoghurt or mayonnaise with the pesto and season.
  2. Lay a slice of cheese on each wrap and divide the chicken between both.
  3. Sprinkle with red pepper or sweetcorn and top with lettuce.
  4. Roll each wrap and pack in lunchbox or wrap in foil.
